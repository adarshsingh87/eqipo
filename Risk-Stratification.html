Risk Stratification

Risk Stratification is a way to quanitfy, track and manage the health and well-being of patient populations for more efficient and effective evaluation and treatment and to ensure consistent quality care for patients. With Equipo, Risk stratification is about strengthening relationships between payers, providers and patients through data transparency, comprehensive documentation and targeted interventions.
Risk Trends More Informative Than Discreet Scoring
The limitations of clinically driven risk modules call for an inventive way to manage risk that provides value to patient populations and healthcare providers. With Risk Analytics from Equipo, both the discreet risk scores as well as the changes in these scores, the risk trends, are provided. Risk trends, rather than the isolated risk scores, are better indicators of care quality or changes in patient conditions.
Pairing Risk - A Better Way To Understand Changes In Patients Outcomes
Risk alone does not provide a full picture of the challenges associated with improving patient care and population outcomes. Equipo enables pairing of risk with other important dimensions so that providers can quickly isolate and understand the factors contributing to changes in patient risk and outcomes. This also allows for better patient triage and prioritization of tasks associated with the care and management of each patient.
Seamless Risk Adjustment and Reconciliation
Documenting and communicating changes in patient risk scores is one way of maintaining the integrity of care in a value based payment model. As comorbidities arise due to patients' age or lifestyle dynamics, conscientious coding and documentation by providers makes for easy reconciliation with payers and gives payers the transparency they need to validate and manage changes in reimbursements. For payers, whose concern it is to ensure the best standard of care for their members, declining risk scores are often indicative of care oversights and result in penalties against providers as a means of motivating them to provide better service.
