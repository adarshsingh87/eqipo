Care Services

We reduce the administrative busy work that drain care managers, diminish continuous care and deplete practice revenues

If care programs are designed to track patient progress and performance, then our care programs equip coordinators to increase patient participation for better outcomes and revenue

More Revenue And Better Outcomes

Our care services lets you engage your existing patient population to increase practice revenue while improving quality scores and patient outcomes.

We've eliminated the drudgery that impedes care manager efficiency by streamlining and automating care programs. Care managers focus only on engaging patients and managing their care programs - positively impacting both the number of participants and their overall adherence.

Simplified Sequences Guide Programs To Completion

We support the cyclical/repetitive and episodic programs driven by Medicare and Managed Care Organizations by ensuring more eligible patients complete them.

Our applications sort patients into their respective program stages, defines the specific activities needed to complete each stage, and enables task assignment so that by focusing on the activities on their workboards, care managers are fulfilling all the obligations needed for patient care.

Track SDOH Trends To Drive Outcomes
Improving outcomes is about defining discrete SDOH measures and tracking and managing overall SDOH trends.

Equipo's screening tools automatically score and trend SDOH measures because showing patient progression over time makes it clear if and where improvements are needed. We also outline the care services and patient engagements needed for preventive measures to avoid costly acute interventions.
