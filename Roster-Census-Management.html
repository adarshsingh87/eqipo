Roster/Census Management
Providers need a more effective way to retrieve and reconcile new patient lists and expedite care plan development across multiple payer networks
Payers portals with dedicated provider logins decentralize patient views for providers and are among the least effective ways to retrieve and reconcile new patient lists for care plan development and patient engagement.
Centralized Visibility And Management Of New Patient Lists
Equipo brokers with different payers to centralizes this process across all integrated payers and all patients providing a single database that consolidates these lists into one centalized dashboard with all NEW patients, regardless of payer for which they are associated. We also enable organization of patients into respective cohorts with prescribed care plans, care gap assessments and patient engagement protocols.
Interventions Supported No Matter Where They Originated
We support both patient-driven and provider-initiated interventions. Our patient facesheets outline patient risk progression, gaps in care and previous interventions so providers can address each of these during visits that are often initiated by patients. We also provide highly flexible cohort views for care coordinators who proactively define and examine different patient populations based on various care dimensions to isolate trends and opportunities to improve care for patient groups.
Continuity And Consistency Of Patient Data
We know that most provider organizations live on their EMRs. For this reason, our application synchronizes with existing EMRs to facilitate a fluid, bilateral flow of patient information across both systems. This ensures data continuity and consistency regardless of where you view the data and avoids rework, copy errors and data-entry by care coordinators and administrators.
