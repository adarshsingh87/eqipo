Provider Organizations

Equipo enables providers to dramatically increase clinical and financial performance in capitated, value-based and risk-based reimbursement models through smart care coordination and patient engagement.

Our advanced data management and analytics deliver scaleble, targeted care plans and illuminate gaps in care. These result in increased revenue, shared savings and improved quality measures.

Key Growth Pillars

QUALITY REPORTING
Real-time quality tracking and streamlined care management workflows optimize shared savings and reimbursements
ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS & ANALYTICS
Care gap analysis and task prioritization improve patient management and keep providers focused on care delivery
RISK-STRATIFIED CARE PLANS

Accountable Care Organizations
Value-based care is marked by improving patient outcomes, closing gaps in patient care and providing preventive interventions that maintain and improve population health all while reducing costs within the healthcare system.

As ACOs migrate to and manage value-based care environment, Equipo helps them improve clinical and financial performance, negotiate better contracts with payors and identify and reconcile discrepencies in quality ratings.
Independent Physician Associations
Equipo enables IPAs to maximize revenue in the value-based landscape by leveraging the physicians network. At the same time, we empower physician-owned practices to manage their own population rosters to ensure patient outcomes.
Health Systems & Hospitals
Hospitals and health systems need a holistic approach to patient care as well as visibility into population trends to better serve the communities in which they reside.

Insights into patient adherence and self-management, post-intervention care transitions and SDOH allow hospital leaders to plan and manage resources to meet the demands of their communities.
Provider and Specialist Groups
Provider and specialist groups can grow their organizations through seamless integration and automation of referral management, prinicipal care, transition care and remote monitoring programs. With Equipo, these programs optimize efficiencies, reimbursements, and patient enrollment and can be customized to your specific practice workflows.
Home Health Agencies
Reputable home health agencies provide quality, reliable care to their patients, establish accountability among their nursing staff and deliver valuable clinical and administrative support to their provider partners.

Equipo’s Case Management tools enable complete process and documentation management for referrals, identify and track billing codes and cycles for accurate and timely claims submission and facilitate easy scheduling, care plan development and coordination.
Skilled Nursing Facilities
An emphasis on quality drives SNF performance in the value-based care landscape and has heightened the demand for quality referral, transition care and case management tools.

The foundation of Equipo’s integrated care coordination, transitional care and referral management solutions is timely, evidence-based, actionable insights. Frontline and back-office teams benefit from up-to-the minute discharge records, centralized admission and facility-initiated H&P notes as well as transparent, quality based care planning and co-ordination.
