Provider Organizations

Equipo's distributed service model enables care through various channels and increases provider revenue in capitated, value-based and risk-based payment and reimbursement models.
Providers can now dramatically increase clinical and financial performance by enabling smart care coordination and patient engagement to develop personalized, targeted care plans and close gaps in care. Our advanced data aggregation and analytics equip providers with the tools they need to drive increases in shared savings and quality reporting, and improve metrics across all payment and reimbursement contracts.

We further maximize improved patient outcomes and provider reimbursements with our risk stratification, SDOH assessments and remote monitoring features that better manage at-risk populations, address obstacles to care, connect patients to the services they need and streamlines management of patients with chronic conditions.
Five Key Pillars
QUALITY REPORTING
Track quality measures in near-real time with automated functionality that optimizes shared savings and reimbursements.
DATA ANALYTICS & ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS
Prioritizing and focusing care delivery where its needed first
RISK-STRATIFIED CARE PLANS
Identify patient cohorts across various disease conditions, develop condition-aligned care programs to eliminate penalties by improving care compliance and identifying risk adjustments
CARE TRANSPARENCY & SHARED DECISION-MAKING
Increase insights into payer ratings and improve reconciliations with integrated patient visibility and understanding of payer scoring methods.
SMART INTERVENTIONS
Use remote patient monitoring and telehealth applications to enable patient education and adherence and improve outcomes
Accountable Care Organizations
Value-based care is marked by improving patient outcomes, closing gaps in patient care and providing preventive interventions that maintain and improve population health all while reducing costs within the healthcare system. Equipo helps ACOs improve clinical and financial performance, negotiate better contracts with payers and identify and reconcile discrepencies in quality ratings as they migrate to and manage the value-based care environment.

Independent Practice Associations
Maximizing revenue and profits while ensuring patient outcomes and maintaining practice autonomy are key objectives for physician-owned practices in the value-based landscape. Equipo enables IPAs to meet these objectives as they transition to value-based contracts with payers.

Health Systems & Hospitals
Hospitals and health systems need a holistic approach to patient care as well as visibility into population trends to better serve the communities in which they reside. Insights into patient adherence and self-management, post-intervention supports and social determinants of health allow hospital leaders to plan and manage resources to meet the demands of their communities.

Provider and Specialist Groups
Provider and specialist groups can grow their organizations through seamlessly integrated and automated referral management, principal and transition care and remote monitoring programs that optimize efficiencies, reimbursements, and patient enrollment.
