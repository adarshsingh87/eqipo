Patient Engagement
With all the forms and IT stuff engineered OUT of the patient engagement process, providers can focus on the tasks that drive continuous care for patients.
Define Specialized Mass Communications
The power of Equipo's smart engagement is that it allows simultaneous, customizeable, automatic outreach to scores of patients in one step. Care coordinators can isolate care groups based on risk, care gaps and other dimensions and then create IVR or SMS messages with dynamic content that changes depending on the patient to whom the message will be sent. Define and create as many groups and unique messages as needed to reach as many patients in one single, automated step.
The Support Of Many
At Equipo, we are passionate about collaboration across the healthcare spectrum. Our obssession with conscientious documentation and visibility drives internal and external efficiency and builds trust for our clients and their partners. We have centralized care coordination dashboards so that multiple care coordinators within a practice can have visibility and access to the tasks, care plans, profiles associated with each patient. With more people looking at and supporting each patients, there are fewer cracks for things to fall through.
Patient Engagement Is A Two-Way Street
Equipo's smart engagement automates patient outreach with virtual assistants and interactive messaging to help close gaps in care and increase efficiency among coordinators. But patient self care (PSC) and responsiveness is an equally important part of patient engagement. From completing forms before visits, to viewing reports and medical histories, to consuming educational content, to patient-provider messaging, the Equipo Engage app makes patient self care possible.
