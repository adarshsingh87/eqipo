Referral Management
Foster real care collaboration by ensuring the same comprehensive view of patient health records to all participating providers so that optimal care can be delivered
Referral Management is about providing and maintaining the same comprehensive view of patient health records to all participating providers so that optimal care can be delivered at every turn. It fosters true collaboration between disparate provider groups and works best when providers on either end of the referral relationship are plugged into the Equipo system.
Patient Facesheets - A Single, Complete View Of Patient Care
The patient facesheet is Equipo's way of streamlining and standardizing all pertinant patient information for detailed, easy communication and distribution within and across provider networks. For integrated provider partners, the facesheets can be accessed on screen. They can also be downloaded and printed, or shared electronically which comes in handy when working with providers not yet integrated with Equipo.
One Flexible Dashboard For Referral Order Simplification
Equipo centralizes all incoming referrals in a single dashboard where referring providers can be isloated, particular specialists assigned, facesheets detailing interactions, conditions, medications associated with each patients accessed and where attachments, forms, communications and any other information that will support a specialist in understanding patient can be shared.
Bi-lateral Transfer of Patient Reports Closes The Loop
It's not enough to send patients to specialists for additional care. Primary care physicians need to document and ensure a clear understanding of the services, diagnoses and outcomes of these referral interactions so that quality continuous care can be maintained. For integrated provider partners, Equipo provides automatic uploads of specialists reports to keep patient files up-to-date and close the loop of care delivery and documentation.
