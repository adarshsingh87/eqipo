CMedicare Advantage
✓ Medicare Advantage (MA) plans are the quintessential example of the demands of value-based reimbursements as their growth is fueled by delivering the highest quality service to their members - in addition to improving care quality, they must also ensure better outcomes and member experiences.

✓ To do this effectively, MA plans require complete visibility into member populations to identify and assess risk trends. These trends drive subsequent activity in the care delivery pipeline - from provider engagement and member outreach, to customized care planning and coordination, to forecasting the future cost of care and supporting plan designs to meet members' changing needs.

✓ With Equipo's strong clinical documentation, real-time visibility and advanced coordination for preventive interventions, MA plans can avoid unnecessary costs and focus on delivering quality, sustainable and relevant services to their members.
Medicaid Managed Care Organizations
Medicaid Managed Care Organizations (MCOs) support the health and wellbeing of the nation’s most vulnerable populations. Not only is it necessary to risk stratify members and develop effective mitigation strategies, but full population visibility with complete views of beneficiary needs as well as a clear understanding of the social determinants of health go a long way to improving accessiblity, engagement, adherence and outcomes within this group.

Equipo care coordination solution has the population health and risk stratification applications that foster strong collaboration between MCOs and Medicaid providers enabling them to cost-effectively improve care quality, accessibility and outcomes.
Self-Insured Employers
Maximizing revenue and profits while ensuring patient outcomes and maintaining practice autonomy are key Attracting and retaining the best employees often comes down to providing competitive and comprehensive healthcare benefits. Employers must do this while simultaneously keeping healthcare costs manageable and increasing employee satisfaction.

Equipo enables visibility into employee healthcare expenses with the actionable insights and tools needed to manage these costs. Our end-to-end care coordination and patient engagement solution allows for risk-stratification of employee populations and cost-based prioritization that drives employee-specific, proactive and preventive care interventions that lower costs and increase satisfaction.
