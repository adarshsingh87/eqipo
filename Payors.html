Payors

Successful payor oversight is a fully collaborative effort between payers, their contracted providers and plan members.

Transitioning to value-based reimbursements is the opportunity for payors to encourage providers to adopt healthcare practices and applications that drive efficiency, transparency and performance.
Equipo's end-to-end care coordination and patient engagement platform offers a full suite of critical care management applications. They help payors control costs and expand member populations by managing the providers who deliver patient care.

Medicare Advantage

Medicare Advantage (MA) plans reflect the central demands of value-based reimbursements. Their growth is fueled by delivering the highest quality service to members and sureing up outcomes and member experiences.

With Equipo's strong clinical documentation, real-time visibility and advanced coordination for preventive interventions, MA plans can avoid unnecessary costs and focus on delivering quality, sustainable and relevant services to their members.

Requirements for Effective MA Plans

1. Complete visibility into member populations
2. Risk identifications and trend analysis
3. Customised, risk-based care plan development and management
4. Care manager engagement and member outreach
5. Care plan forecasting - future cost of care, plan design and member needs

Self-Insured Employers

Attracting and retaining the best employees often comes down to providing competitive and comprehensive healthcare benefits. Employers must do this while simultaneously keeping healthcare costs manageable.

Equipo enables visibility into employee healthcare expenses and provides actionable insights that help employers manage these costs.

Our end-to-end care coordination and patient engagement solution allows for risk-stratification of employee populations and cost-based prioritization that drives employee-specific, preventive interventions that lower costs and increase satisfaction.
